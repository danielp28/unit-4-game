Crystals Collector

You will given a random number at the start of the game

There are four skate logos below. By clicking on a logo you will add a specific amount of point to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number given, you lose if your score goes above the random number.

The value of each logo is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the logos are given a new value.
